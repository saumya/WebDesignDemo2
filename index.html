How Our Story Began
Saurabh Malpani
Founder & CEO
Rima Bhattad
Co-Founder

At Malpani Mangoes our mission is to help farmers get the best value for their products and our customers get authentic and natural produce directly from the people who grow it.

Devgad is known for the world's best mangoes but its' growers(the farmers) are still not able to reach the cities in India and consumers too do not get the original "Devgad" because they are lost in the market full of dummy mangoes. Farmers spend enormous efforts in Plucking, Grading, Packing, and transportation of mangoes during the scorching heat of summer. So they do not connect with the customers in the limited time and even if they do, customers bargain bigtime with farmers demotivating them in doing sales in the Indian cities. We are also committed to stop the harassment by cutting the middlemen and give the maximum share of profit in farmers hand.
